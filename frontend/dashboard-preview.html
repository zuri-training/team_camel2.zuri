This Guide gives a brief introduction to common wealth privacy law for those small Businesses that need to
comply with the Privacy Act 1988. The guide does not describe the law in detail. it
is intended to provide useful pointers.

Small businesses may wish to look at other information produced by the Office of the Privacy
Commissioner
or to seek legal or other advice if they are unsure about what the
privacy Act
requires in more complex cases.
